Teaching philosophy

Grounded, gentle, and accessible

Bindu’s yoga offerings are centered on care, breath, and mindful movement. Her classes are designed to feel welcoming, whether someone is brand new to yoga or returning to practice.

She teaches with an emphasis on comfort, body awareness, and creating a peaceful space for students to reconnect with themselves.
