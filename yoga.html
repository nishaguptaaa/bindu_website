Pranayama

Breath meditation that relaxes and revitalizes

Pranayama is the conscious awareness of the breath — the life force that energizes and relaxes the body. In class, students explore traditional yogic breathing practices designed to support calm, concentration, and overall well-being.

These sessions teach proper breathing techniques and relaxation methods that can be incorporated into daily life, along with simple stretches that are easy to follow and practice at home.

Benefits of practice

Supports concentration and focus

Encourages deeper rest and relaxation

Helps reduce stress and tension

Supports physical, mental, and emotional well-being
