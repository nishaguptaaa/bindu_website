About Bindu

Warm, present, and deeply rooted in tradition

Bindu offers yoga, Reiki, and creative experiences that help people feel grounded, calm, and connected. Her teaching is rooted in the authentic lineage of yoga — not as a fitness trend, but as a complete philosophy of living.

Students consistently describe her as calm, knowledgeable, detailed, and deeply encouraging. Whether guiding a group class, offering a private Reiki session, or leading a dot mandala workshop, her goal is always the same: to help you feel supported and at home in your own body.

Her Philosophy

Yoga is not a workout

In a world where yoga has been reduced to a fitness class, Bindu offers something different — a practice that honors its origins. She brings deep cultural knowledge and genuine reverence for the tradition to every session, teaching asana alongside pranayama, philosophy, and the quieter dimensions of the practice.

She is especially passionate about making this authentic practice accessible to people who have felt that wellness spaces were not meant for them. They are. You are welcome here, exactly as you are.
