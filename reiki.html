About Reiki

What Is Reiki?

Reiki is a Japanese healing art that channels universal life energy to support the recipient's physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. It is deeply relaxing, always gentle, and completely non-invasive.

During a session, you will lie fully clothed in a comfortable setting. Bindu works with your energy field — no touch required — allowing energy to flow where it is most needed. Most people experience warmth, deep relaxation, and a profound sense of peace. Many fall asleep.

Who Is It For?

Open to Everyone

Reiki is suitable for anyone regardless of age or health status. It is particularly supportive for those experiencing stress and anxiety, chronic tension, fatigue, emotional overwhelm, or anyone seeking a space for quiet inner restoration.

Bindu is happy to discuss whether Reiki may be a good fit for you before booking.
