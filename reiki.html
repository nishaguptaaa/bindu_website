What Is Reiki

Ancient healing, modern presence

Reiki is a Japanese healing art that channels universal life energy to support the recipient's physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. It is deeply relaxing, always gentle, and completely non-invasive.

During a session with Bindu, you will lie fully clothed in a comfortable setting. Working with your energy field — no touch required — Bindu allows energy to flow where it is most needed. Most people experience warmth, a profound sense of peace, and deep, restorative rest.

Who It Is For

Open to everyone

Reiki is suitable for anyone regardless of age or health status. It is particularly supportive for those experiencing stress and anxiety, chronic tension, fatigue, emotional overwhelm, or anyone seeking a quiet space for inner restoration. Bindu is always happy to speak with you before booking to answer questions and help you choose the right session.
