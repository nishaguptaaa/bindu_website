About Bindu

A practice of mindfulness and creativity

Bindu’s work brings together calm, care, and self-expression. Through both yoga and art, she creates spaces where people can slow down, reconnect, and feel supported.

Her teaching style is warm, knowledgeable, and approachable, with a focus on accessibility, relaxation, and meaningful growth.
