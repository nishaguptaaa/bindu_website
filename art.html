The Practice

One dot at a time

Dots With Bindu is a meditative art practice rooted in the same principles that guide Bindu's yoga and Reiki work — presence, patience, and intention. Each piece is created one dot at a time, one breath at a time.

The work draws from sacred geometry, the natural world, and the quiet space between thoughts. Whether intimate or large-scale, each piece carries the energy of the moment it was made.

Commissions & Workshops

Bring a piece home

Original pieces and custom commissions are available. Bindu also leads dot mandala workshops at local libraries and community spaces — a joyful, meditative experience for all skill levels. See the Classes & Events page for upcoming workshop dates, or reach out to inquire about a commission.
