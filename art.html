About the Work

The Art of the Dot

Dots With Bindu is a meditative art practice — each piece created one dot at a time. The process itself is a form of meditation, requiring presence and patience in equal measure.

Bindu's work is inspired by nature, sacred geometry, and the quiet moments between breaths. Her pieces range from intimate originals to larger commissions.
